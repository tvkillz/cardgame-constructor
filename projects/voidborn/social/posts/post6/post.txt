**Domain Spotlight: Kronos**

The earth is our armor. Kronos stands as the primary bulwark against the Void, utilizing ancient geology and root-magic to halt the Aether Bleed in its tracks.

- **High Defense:** Specializes in tanks and high-health units.
- **Siege Warfare:** Dominates the battlefield with slow-moving but devastating impact units.
- **Golden Resilience:** Utilizes amber-infused artifacts to buff defenses.
