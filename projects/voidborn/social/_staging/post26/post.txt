Within the burning skies of the Infernus realm, few creatures possess the speed and intelligence of the Ash Wing Scout. As a crucial scout for the elemental forces, these units serve as the first line of offense against the encroaching Void corruption. 

Mechanically, the Ash Wing Scout is a dangerous 2-mana, 2/1 presence that demands an answer. Its unique 'Aerial Ember' ability ensures that it cannot be blocked by ground-based units, forcing your opponent to reach for their own aerial defenders. Furthermore, the capacity to draw a card upon dealing combat damage makes it an essential engine for maintaining momentum in your deck.
